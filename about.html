Our mission

The UVT Peer Tutoring Hub was created with a simple goal: to make learning more collaborative and less stressful. We believe that the best way to master difficult concepts is by discussing them with other people who have faced the same difficulties.

Who are we?

We are a community of university students across various faculties, from Computer Science to Mathematics, volunteering our time to help others. Our tutors are senior students who have gotten the best grades across all years of studies.

How it works

It's very simple to get involved:

  • Step 1: Check the Schedule page to find a study group for your subject.
  • Step 2: Go to the Join Us page and fill out the registration form.
  • Step 3: Show up, ask questions, and learn together!
